Sponge Cake

Description

Learn how to do a wonderful and easy homemade orange flavored sponge cake with just a few ingredients, ideal for your every-morning breakfast!

Ingredients

  • Eggs: 2
  • Sugar: 150gr
  • Vanilla extract: a splash
  • Orange (to grate the peel): 1
  • Self-raising flour: 250gr
  • Baking powder: a tea spoon
  • Butter or oil: enough to cover your cake pan

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven at low fire before starting the recipe.
  2. Whip the eggs with the sugar.
  3. Add the vanilla extract and the grated orange.
  4. Sift the dry ingredients and add them to the wet ingredients, mixing in a 8 shape movement.
  5. Prepared a cake pan with the butter or oil, covering the bottom and walls of it and add a little bit of flour.
  6. Bake at medium fire for about 30 minutes or until your knife inserted into the middle comes out clean
  7. Feel free of decorate it with cream or fruits or whatever you like. Enjoy! :D
